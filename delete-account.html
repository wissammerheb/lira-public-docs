Delete your Lira account

Last updated: March 8, 2026

This page explains how users of Lira can request deletion of their account and associated data.

How to delete your account (in the app)

  1. Open the Lira app.
  2. Go to Settings.
  3. In the Account section, tap Delete account.
  4. Confirm the deletion prompt.

If deletion fails with a security message, sign out and sign in again, then retry.

Data deleted

When you delete your account from the app, we delete:

  • Your Firebase Authentication account used to sign in.
  • Your user profile document in our database (for example: name, email, preferences, and notification token stored under your user ID).
  • Your membership count contribution in your family group (if applicable).

Data that may be kept

If you used Lira in a shared family group, expense/category records you previously created in that shared group may remain for accounting/history continuity for other family members. These records may include information you entered (for example amount, description, and display name saved on an expense entry).

If you want those shared records removed, delete them in the app before account deletion, or contact us.

Retention period

Account/profile deletion triggered in-app is processed immediately. Limited operational backups/logs may persist for a short period according to infrastructure retention policies before permanent removal.
